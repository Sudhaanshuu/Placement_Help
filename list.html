LeetCode

Primarily targets individuals preparing for coding interviews, particularly those aiming for positions at tech giants like Google, Facebook, Amazon, and Microsoft. Its community is vast, with millions of users worldwide, including beginners, intermediate programmers, and professionals. LeetCode offers a wide range of problems, from easy to hard, covering various domains such as arrays, strings, linked lists, trees, graphs, dynamic programming, etc. The platform emphasizes performance metrics, such as runtime and memory usage, encouraging participants to optimize their solutions.
